Instructions

Below the line is a copy of the controls for the main page (they dont do anything here).


The page loads in Solver Mode, where all you do is put in a word for it to solve, and hit play!

Once a word is solved you can use the Copy button to copy the results to your clipboard

You can also use the Toggle Letters button to hide the letters in the grid, only showing colors


The Show/Hide Log button is where the algorithm dispenses its analysis of the word and possible guesses. It shows up below the playing grid.

The Show/Hide Settings button will allow you to change the algorithm's start word and toggle Hard Mode rules!


You can have the AI analyze your guesses with Interactive Mode!

When interactive mode is enabled and you press Play!, the starting guess will be entered and then you get to play.

After each guess the AI will show how many possibilities remain in the log.

You can undo a guess and try a different word to see how good different guesses are.


The Score Distribution button runs an analysis of all Wordle games with the given starting word and dispenses the results in the log.

Warning: Running the Score Distribution will take upwards of 30 minutes when not using Hard Mode rules!


Thats all the instructions, have fun!
